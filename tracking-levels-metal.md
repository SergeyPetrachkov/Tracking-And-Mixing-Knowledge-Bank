## Core Philosophy
- Record **clean, dynamic, unclipped** signals
- Leave **headroom** for mixing and mastering
- Do NOT chase loudness while tracking
- Plugins work best around **-18 dBFS RMS**

Target rule of thumb:
- **Peaks:** -10 to -6 dBFS
- **Average (RMS):** ~ -18 dBFS
- Never hit 0 dBFS

### DI Tracking
- Peaks: **-10 to -6 dBFS**
- RMS: **-18 to -14 dBFS**
- No compression
- No EQ
- Cleanest signal possible

### Amp / Fractal Printed Tracks
- Peaks: **-8 to -6 dBFS**
- RMS: **-18 to -14 dBFS**
- Avoid limiter or maximizer
- Saturation ONLY if part of tone

### Notes
- Quad tracking = consistency > loudness
- Avoid clipping transients; metal guitars still need attack
- Slightly quieter is always safer
