# Mastering Metal – FabFilter-Based Workflow

## Pre-Master Requirements
- Mix bus peaks: **-6 dBFS**
- No clipping
- No limiter on mix bus
- Good balance before mastering

---

## Mastering Chain

### 1. EQ – FabFilter Pro-Q
- HPF: 25–30 Hz
- Tiny corrective cuts only
- Use Mid/Side carefully
- Never “mix” in mastering

---

### 2. Compression – Softube VCA
- Ratio: 2:1
- Attack: slow (30 ms)
- Release: Auto
- GR: 1–2 dB

---

### 3. Multiband Control – FabFilter Pro-MB
- Low band (40–120 Hz)
  - Ratio: 1.5–2:1
  - GR: 1–2 dB
- High mids (2–5 kHz)
  - Control harshness only if needed

---

### 4. Saturation (Optional)
- Decapitator
- Drive: 0.5–1
- Mix: 5–10%

---

### 5. Limiting – FabFilter Pro-L

Settings:
- Style: Modern or Aggressive
- Lookahead: Default
- Ceiling: **-1.0 dBTP**
- Oversampling: On

### Loudness Targets
- Modern metal: **-8 to -6 LUFS**
- Dynamic metal: **-10 to -8 LUFS**

---

## Final Checks
- Mono compatibility
- Low volume balance
- Headphones vs speakers
- No pumping, no fizz
